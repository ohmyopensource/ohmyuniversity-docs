# 2 Current System

The current university digital ecosystem is characterized by significant information fragmentation. Students are forced to navigate between obsolete institutional portals, static PDF files for schedules, and various physical offices. The following section provides a detailed analysis of current systems and key competitors to highlight the added value of OhMyUniversity.


| Category | **APP UNIMOL** | **UNIWHERE** | **UNIDAYS** | **THEFACULTY** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profile** | Official Institutional System (Esse3/Cineca). | Third-party academic aggregator. | Lifestyle & shopping discount platform. | Student gamification and rewards. |
| **Key Objectives** | Administrative management (fees, enrollments, exams). | Simplify the visualization of the academic career. | Provide exclusive commercial discounts to members. | Engage students through quizzes and prize challenges. |
| **Strategy** | Digitization of mandatory University bureaucratic processes. | Freemium model based on modern UX and career statistics. | Global brand partnerships for student savings. | Learning gamification to offer commercial coupons. |
| **Market Advantage** | Official status and direct access to administrative data. | Clean interface and multi-university support. | Global discount database and rapid verification. | High engagement through rankings and community challenges. |
| **Strengths** | Institutional security and guaranteed data integrity. | Excellent User Experience and data visualization. | Vast range of discounts and international reach. | Unique playful approach and merit-based rewards. |
| **Weaknesses** | Complex navigation and lack of logistical services. | Lack of deep local integration (canteen) and teaching materials. | Zero utility for academic career or planning. | Limited or absent academic utility features. |
| **UX/UI** | Institutional standard, not very user-oriented. | Modern, minimalist, and highly responsive. | E-commerce layout, retail-oriented. | Dynamic interface rich in animations. |


While current systems focus either on pure administration (**App UniMol**) or on basic career tracking (**Uniwhere**), OhMyUniversity addresses the critical gaps identified in the Requirements Analysis Document:

* **Logistical Support**: Real-time management of **canteens, classrooms, and transportation**, which is absent in other solutions.
* **Strategic Planning**: Automated verification of requirements for **Master's degrees**, replacing the manual review of static PDF files.
* **Operational Efficiency**: A target for retrieving critical information in **3 interactions (taps) or fewer**, countering the complexity of current portals.
