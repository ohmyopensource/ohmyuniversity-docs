# 2 Current System

The current university digital ecosystem is characterized by significant information fragmentation[cite: 1]. Students are forced to navigate between obsolete institutional portals, static PDF files for schedules, and various physical offices[cite: 1]. The following section provides a detailed analysis of current systems and key competitors to highlight the added value of OhMyUniversity.


| Category | **APP UNIMOL** | **UNIWHERE** | **UNIDAYS** | **THEFACULTY** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profile** | Official Institutional System (Esse3/Cineca)[cite: 1]. | Third-party academic aggregator. | Lifestyle & shopping discount platform. | Student gamification and rewards. |
| **Key Objectives** | Administrative management (fees, enrollments, exams)[cite: 1]. | Simplify the visualization of the academic career. | Provide exclusive commercial discounts to members. | Engage students through quizzes and prize challenges. |
| **Strategy** | Digitization of mandatory University bureaucratic processes[cite: 1]. | Freemium model based on modern UX and career statistics. | Global brand partnerships for student savings. | Learning gamification to offer commercial coupons. |
| **Market Advantage** | Official status and direct access to administrative data[cite: 1]. | Clean interface and multi-university support. | Global discount database and rapid verification. | High engagement through rankings and community challenges. |
| **Strengths** | Institutional security and guaranteed data integrity[cite: 1]. | Excellent User Experience and data visualization. | Vast range of discounts and international reach. | Unique playful approach and merit-based rewards. |
| **Weaknesses** | Complex navigation and lack of logistical services[cite: 1]. | Lack of deep local integration (canteen) and teaching materials[cite: 1]. | Zero utility for academic career or planning. | Limited or absent academic utility features. |
| **UX/UI** | Institutional standard, not very user-oriented[cite: 1]. | Modern, minimalist, and highly responsive. | E-commerce layout, retail-oriented. | Dynamic interface rich in animations. |


While current systems focus either on pure administration (**App UniMol**) or on basic career tracking (**Uniwhere**), OhMyUniversity addresses the critical gaps identified in the Requirements Analysis Document[cite: 1]:

* **Logistical Support**: Real-time management of **canteens, classrooms, and transportation**, which is absent in other solutions[cite: 1].
* **Strategic Planning**: Automated verification of requirements for **Master's degrees**, replacing the manual review of static PDF files[cite: 1].
* **Operational Efficiency**: A target for retrieving critical information in **3 interactions (taps) or fewer**, countering the complexity of current portals[cite: 1].
