# Overview

OhMyUniversity! is a centralized university platform designed to simplify the interaction between students and the digital services offered by the university. The system provides both a Web application, developed in Angular, and a Mobile application, developed in Flutter.

The goal of the platform is to reduce the fragmentation of existing university systems by offering a single access point to academic, administrative, didactic, and organizational services. Through OhMyUniversity!, students can consult their academic career, view exams and grades, check their study plan, access exam sessions, consult university services, manage calendar events, and use orientation-related tools.

The platform integrates, or is designed to integrate, with existing university systems such as Esse3/Cineca for academic career data and Moodle or other external services for didactic materials and institutional resources.

For this reason, the quality of the system depends not only on the correctness of the internal implementation, but also on the reliability of external services and on the ability of the platform to handle unavailable or delayed data sources.
