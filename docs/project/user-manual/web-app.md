# Web Guide

## Accessing the Platform

1. Open the login page.
2. Select your university.
3. Enter your institutional email address or username.
4. Enter your password.
5. Press Login.
6. After logging in, you are redirected to the dashboard.

## Viewing Academic Career Data

1. Enter the dashboard.
2. Open the Career section.
3. View exams, grades, CFU credits, arithmetic average, weighted average, and graduation base grade.

## Viewing the Study Plan

1. Open the Study Plan section.
2. View the planned courses.
3. Check the status of the exams.

## Viewing Exam Sessions and Bookings

1. Open the Exam Sessions section.
2. View the available exam sessions.
3. Check dates, times, classroom, and booking status.

## Viewing Tuition Fees and Administrative Documents

1. Open the Student Office section.
2. Check the tuition fee status.
3. View available calls, forms, or documents.

## Accessing External Services

1. Open the Portals or Services section.
2. Select Moodle, institutional email, library, or Esse3.
3. The system opens the selected service.

## Using the Calendar

1. Open the Agenda section.
2. View events, deadlines, and commitments.
3. Add or edit an event if the feature is available.

## Using the Orientation Guide

1. Open the Orientation section.
2. Choose a topic.
3. Read the guide and answer the questions.
4. View the final summary.
