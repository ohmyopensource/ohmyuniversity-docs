# FAQ

## How is the average calculated?

The arithmetic average considers the recorded grades. The weighted average also takes into account the CFU credits associated with the exams.

## Is the graduation base grade final?

No. It is an estimate calculated using the available academic career data.

## Why can’t I see an exam?

The exam may not have been synchronized with the university system yet.

## Can I use Moodle from the app?

Yes. The system provides direct access to the Moodle service configured for the university.

## Does the digital badge always replace the physical one?

It depends on the university’s rules. The digital badge displays the student’s identification data.
