# Troubleshooting

## Invalid Credentials

Check the username/email, password, and selected university. If the problem continues, try again later.

## Session Expired

Log in again from the login screen.

## Academic Career Data Not Available

The university service may be temporarily unreachable. Try again after a few minutes.

## Exam Sessions Not Loaded

Check your connection and try again. If the problem continues, the Esse3 service may not be available.

## External Service Unreachable

Moodle, email, library, or the student portal may depend on external systems. In case of an error, try again later.

## Calendar Not Updated

Refresh the page or reopen the app. If the event does not appear, check your connection.
