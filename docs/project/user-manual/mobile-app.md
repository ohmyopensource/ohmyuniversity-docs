# Mobile Guide

## Accessing the App

1. Open the OhMyUniversity app.
2. Enter your university, username/email, and password.
3. Tap Login.
4. After logging in, you will see the dashboard.

## Quickly Viewing Academic Career Data

1. Tap Career in the bottom navigation bar.
2. View averages, CFU credits, exams, and progress.
3. Open a course to view its details.

## Viewing the Study Plan

1. Open the Career or Teaching section.
2. Tap Study Plan.
3. View the planned courses and their status.

## Viewing Exam Sessions

1. Open the Exam Sessions section.
2. Filter or scroll through the available exam sessions.
3. Check the date, time, classroom, and status.

## Showing the Digital Badge

1. Tap the profile section.
2. Open the digital badge.
3. Show the badge when requested.

## Using University Services

1. Open the services menu.
2. Tap Moodle, Email, Library, or Student Portal.
3. The app opens the selected service.

## Viewing Class Schedules and Calendar

1. Open Class Schedule to view the available PDFs.
2. Open Calendar to view commitments and deadlines.
3. Add or edit events if the feature is available.

## Using Orientation

1. Open Orientation.
2. Choose a topic.
3. Read the information and answer the questions.
4. Go to the final summary.
